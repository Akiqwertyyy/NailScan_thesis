## 📱 WHAT THE APP DOES

**NailScan** is an AI-powered mobile app that:

✅ Takes photos of fingernails
✅ Analyzes them using AI (mock data included)
✅ Provides detailed diagnosis results
✅ Tracks scan history
✅ Displays medical information

### Detects 5 Conditions:
1. Onychomycosis (fungal infection)
2. Acral Lentiginous Melanoma (ALM)
3. Nail Clubbing
4. Healthy Nail
5. Unidentified

### 7 Complete Screens:
1. **Splash** - Animated intro (2.5s)
2. **Home** - Main dashboard
3. **Capture** - Camera/gallery picker
4. **Processing** - AI loading animation
5. **Result** - Detailed diagnosis
6. **History** - Past scans
7. **About** - App information
